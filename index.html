YOUNG SURVEYORS NETWORK OF NIGERIA (YSN) PRESENTS GIS DAY, JOS PLATEAU EXPERIENCE

Participate in the international celebration of GIS technology. GIS is a scientific framework for gathering, analyzing and visualizing geographic data to help us make better decision. On GIS Day, we help people learn about the application of GIS technology to develop solutions for real-world problems and making a difference in our society. The GIS Day in Plateau State is organised by The Young Surveyors Network of Nigeria (YSN) Plateau State chapter in partnership with Datahub360.

  • Our theme for this years GIS Day is:

  • “Geospatial Technology as a tool for National Development the Plateau Experience”

  • Date: 18th Novemeber 2020

  • Time: 1:00pm (WAT)

  • VENUE: PLATEAU STATE, MINISTRY FOR LANDS, SURVEY & TOWN PLANNING, JOS.
